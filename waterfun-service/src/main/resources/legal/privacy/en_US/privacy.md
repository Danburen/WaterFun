# WaterFun Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 1, 2026

Effective Date: July 1, 2026

Welcome to WaterFun. We take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide

When you register an account, you provide your phone number, email address, username, and password. When completing your profile, you may voluntarily upload an avatar, set a nickname, and write a bio. When you publish content, posts, comments, images, and other information you make public will be collected and stored.

### 1.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you access and use our platform, we automatically collect device information including model, operating system version, and device identifiers; log data including IP address, browser type, access time, and browsing history; and network information including network type and carrier.

### 1.3 Third-Party Information

When you log in using a third-party account, we may collect your public information from that platform, including username and avatar, subject to your authorization. The specific scope of collection is determined by the third party's authorization page.

## 2. How We Use Information

We use collected information to create and maintain your account; provide community features including posts, comments, likes, and bookmarks; send system notifications and alerts; continuously optimize platform experience and improve service quality; ensure account security by detecting and preventing violations; and comply with legal obligations.

## 3. Data Storage

### 3.1 Storage Location

Your data is stored on servers within the People's Republic of China. Should cross-border data transmission become necessary due to business needs, we will strictly comply with applicable laws and regulations.

### 3.2 Retention Period

We retain your personal information for the duration of your account. Upon account deletion, we will delete or anonymize your personal information in accordance with legal requirements.

## 4. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may share your information under the following circumstances: with your explicit consent; when required by law or government authorities; or when necessary to protect the legitimate rights of WaterFun or other users.

## 5. Your Rights

You have the right to access and correct your personal information; delete your personal information; withdraw your consent; delete your account; and file complaints. You may exercise these rights through your account settings page or by contacting our support team.

## 6. Minor Protection

We take the protection of minors' personal information seriously. If you are under 18 years of age, please use our platform under the guidance of a legal guardian. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors.

## 7. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. In the event of material changes, we will notify you through platform announcements, internal messages, or email. The updated policy takes effect on the date specified in the notice.
